BreathEasy-ISHA team 1 is a healthcare startup created as part of a college project to provide affordable and accessible medical imaging services to patients. Our team consists of healthcare professionals, software engineers, and data scientists who aim to revolutionize the healthcare industry using the latest technology. We have developed a platform that connects patients with local imaging centers to make medical imaging services such as CT scans and X-rays more accessible and affordable. We believe in timely and accurate medical imaging to detect and treat diseases, and our platform is designed to make it easier for patients to receive the care they need. Our mission is to provide high-quality, reliable medical imaging services that are accessible to all patients, regardless of their financial situation. We strive to achieve this goal by ensuring transparency, accountability, and exceptional customer service. We are dedicated to providing the best possible care to our patients and improving their quality of life.
